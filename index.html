    Academics

    Here you learn how to really be a student. You study, build projects, do presentations, cooperate in teams, win in competitions and make a new family. Yeah, that's really cool. Here you, for the first time, will be staying in dorms with your closest friends. Unlimited number of stories, laughs, challenges, stay-ups, but most importantly, Studying together. Not forgetting that this IS a school, you must be studying. Here you will encounter how teams work together to make the best outcome. You study together, you prepare together, you help eachother, all other..other..other. Humans are meant to help. And STEMers fulfil this mean

    Because you are special, we prepared specialsyllabus for you. As our students have the ability to do more and recieve more, our cirriculums targets and works on this abilities to thrive. You study more advanced science subjects and topics than any other high school student in Egypt. Our teached lessons include a variety of topics, that, for sure, will capture you interest. So, get excited, future STEMer!!
